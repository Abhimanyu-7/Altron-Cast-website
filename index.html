Who We Are

A Legacy of Precision in Die Casting

Altron Cast Pvt. Ltd. has been at the forefront of precision die casting for over 6 years, delivering high-quality aluminium components to automotive and non-automotive industries. Based out of IMT Manesar, Gurugram, we combine technical expertise with a passion for quality to serve clients across India.
