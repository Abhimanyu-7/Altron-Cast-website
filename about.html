Company Overview

Precision Die Casting, Engineered for Industry

Altron Cast Pvt. Ltd. was established with a singular vision — to deliver world-class die casting solutions to both the automotive and non-automotive sectors. Operating from our state-of-the-art facility in IMT Manesar, Gurugram, we have spent 7 years refining our processes, expanding our capabilities, and building lasting partnerships with our clients.

We specialize in aluminium and zinc pressure die casting, producing components that meet stringent quality standards. From concept to delivery, our team of engineers and technicians ensure that every part that leaves our facility represents the best in precision manufacturing.

Our facility is equipped with modern die casting machines, in-house tooling capabilities, and a dedicated quality control lab — enabling us to maintain complete control over the production lifecycle.
